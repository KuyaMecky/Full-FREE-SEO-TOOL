General Questions
What is Philotech and what services do you offer?
Philotech is a next-generation MSP that helps businesses manage, secure, and optimize their IT environments. Our core solutions include PhiloSecure for cybersecurity and disaster recovery, and PhiloWork for managed IT — supported by 24/7 helpdesk and compliance management.
Who can benefit from Philotech's services?
Philotech is ideal for small to mid-sized businesses seeking to strengthen IT performance, cybersecurity, and compliance. Our solutions scale to match your business needs and growth.
Why should SMBs care about cybersecurity?
Over 60% of cyberattacks now focus on SMBs because they often have weaker defenses. A single breach can lead to data loss, revenue loss, regulatory fines, and even business closure.
How quickly can Philotech detect and stop an attack?
Most threats are detected and mitigated within minutes rather than hours or days, with expert MDR analysts minimizing false positives and unnecessary impact to your operations.
Pricing & Billing
Do you charge setup or onboarding fees?
No. Setup, onboarding, and basic configuration are included in your chosen plan with no hidden charges.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes. Plans are flexible — you can scale up or down as your business grows. Our team assists with all transitions to ensure uninterrupted service.
Technology & Terminology
What is MDR (Managed Detection and Response)?
MDR is a managed cybersecurity service that monitors your systems 24/7, detects suspicious activities, and responds to threats automatically or through expert analysts.
What is the difference between backup and disaster recovery?
Backup is the process of saving copies of your data. Disaster Recovery is the plan and tools used to restore systems and data after a major incident. Both are included in all PhiloSecure and PhiloWork plans.
